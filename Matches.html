The Game of Matchboxes

This game gives you an opportunity to choose:
1) overall number of matches in the matchbox: 25;
2) max. number of matches that player can take from the matchbox: 3.
You will take the first turn.
Click one of the buttons with numbers to take corresponding number of matches.
If you want to play the alternative mode, click the "Alternative Game" button.
