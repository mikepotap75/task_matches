Second Game

This game gives you an opportunity to choose:
1) overall number of matches in the matchbox (odd number, >=3);
2) max. number of matches that player can take from the matchbox (>=1).
(You can alter both numbers by clicking on arrows in their fields).
Computer will take the first turn.
To start the game, click the "Generate Matches" button.
Click one of the buttons with numbers to take corresponding number of matches.
Click the "Main Game" button, if you want to return to the previous mode.
